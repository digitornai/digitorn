# Billing & Subscriptions

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Seats | AI credits / mo |
|------|-------|-------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | 3 | 100 |
| Pro | $12 / seat / mo | unlimited | 2,000 |
| Business | $24 / seat / mo | unlimited | 10,000 |

## Changing your plan

- **Upgrade**: takes effect immediately; the new rate is pro-rated for the
  remaining days of the cycle.
- **Downgrade**: takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Unused
  value is credited to your balance.

## Invoices & receipts

All invoices are available under **Settings → Billing → History**. A PDF
receipt is emailed automatically after each charge.

## Failed payments

If a charge fails, we retry 3 times over 7 days. The workspace is set to
read-only after the 4th failure until billing is fixed. No data is deleted.

## Tax

Prices exclude VAT/GST. Business customers can add a tax ID under
**Settings → Billing → Tax** to receive a tax-compliant invoice.
