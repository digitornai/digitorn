# Refund Policy

## Eligibility

Lumina offers a **30-day money-back guarantee** on all paid plans. A refund can
be requested if:

- The request is made within 30 days of the original payment.
- The account has not violated our Terms of Service.
- For annual plans, no more than 3 team members were active during the period.

## What is NOT refundable

- Add-on usage already consumed (extra storage, AI credits).
- Payments older than 30 days.
- Accounts suspended for abuse.

## How refunds are processed

1. Support verifies the order number and payment date.
2. Refunds above **$200** require **human approval** before they are issued.
3. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within
   5–10 business days.

## Pro-rated refunds

Downgrades mid-cycle are credited (not cash-refunded) to the account balance
and applied to the next invoice.
